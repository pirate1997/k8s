1. pod创建后自动使用pvc关联pv，不受重启影响，删除pod后，pvc pv都不会删除
   创建后的pod资深的hostnam会在dns注册
[root@k8s01 statefulset]# kubectl exec -ti myapp-0 -- /bin/sh
/ # nslookup myapp-0.svc-stateful-myapp.default.svc.cluster.local
nslookup: can't resolve '(null)': Name does not resolve

Name:      myapp-0.svc-stateful-myapp.default.svc.cluster.local
Address 1: 10.244.2.124 myapp-0.svc-stateful-myapp.default.svc.cluster.local
2. statefulset也支持扩容
[root@k8s01 statefulset]# kubectl scale sts myapp --replicas=3
statefulset.apps/myapp scaled
