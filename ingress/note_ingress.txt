创建https服务
1.创建私有key
  openssl genrsa -out tls.key 2048

2.自签证书
  openssl req -new -x509 -key tls.key -out tls.crt -subj /C=CN/ST=BeiJing/L=Beijing/O=devops/CN=a.kk.com

3.创建资源对象secret
  kubectl create secret tls myapp-ingress-secret --cert=tls.crt --key=tls.key 
  kubectl get secrets 
  kubectl describe secret myapp-ingress-secret

4.创建tls类型ingress
  cp ingress-myapp.yaml ingress-myapp-tls.yaml 
  ingress-myapp-tls.yaml 

5.测试https服务

└─$ curl https://a.kk.com:30948 -k
